Sacred Pilgrimage Spots

Kashi Vishwanath Temple 1–2 hours

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, the golden spire and sacred energy attract millions. We ensure quick darshan & guided visit.

Tulsi Manas Temple

Dedicated to Lord Ram, this white marble temple showcases verses of the Ramcharitmanas. Peaceful & architectural beauty.

Shri Kaal Bhairav Temple

Guardian deity of Varanasi. Traditional rituals & unique customs — a powerful spiritual stop.

Varanasi's Vibrant Markets & Local Food

Explore the chaotic lanes of Vishwanath Gali, taste famous Kashi chaat, malaiyo, and thandai. An authentic local experience.

Optional: Kachori sabzi, Banarasi paan

Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat 1–2 hours

Witness the world-famous spiritual ceremony — priests with huge lamps, mantras, and divine energy as dusk falls. A soul-stirring spectacle.
