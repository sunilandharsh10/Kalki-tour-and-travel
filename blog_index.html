Ayodhya Birthplace of Lord Rama

Ayodhya, along the Sarayu River, holds the magnificent Ram Janmabhoomi Temple — a three-story Nagara-style marvel with 392 pillars & 44 doors. The sacred Sarayu Ghats offer divine aarti and peaceful boat rides.

  • Ram Mandir Darshan
  • Saryu Aarti
  • Guide & Boat booking

Varanasi (Kashi) City Older Than Time

Experience timeless magic: Ganga Aarti at dusk, early morning boat rides on the Ganges, and labyrinthine alleyways filled with incense & chants. Kashi Sangam crafts soulful journeys through vibrant ghats and ancient temples.

  • Divine Ganga Aarti
  • Sunrise Boat Ride
  • Temple tours (Kashi Vishwanath)

Prayagraj Triveni Sangam

Holy confluence of Ganga, Yamuna & mythical Saraswati. Taking a sacred dip at Sangam is believed to wash away sins. Famous for the Kumbh Mela — the world's largest spiritual gathering — and ancient temples nearby.

  • Sangam Snan
  • Kumbh Mela experience
  • Complete travel support
