The Divine Circuit — Suggested Pilgrimage

Day 1: Varanasi

Morning boat ride on Ganga, Kashi Vishwanath darshan, Sarnath excursion, evening Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh.

Day 2: Prayagraj

Drive to Prayagraj (3 hrs). Triveni Sangam boat ride, Akshayavat, Hanuman Mandir, and witness the confluence magic.

Day 3: Ayodhya

Morning departure to Ayodhya (4 to 5 hrs). Ram Janmabhoomi, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhavan, Sarayu evening aarti. and Tour ended here.
