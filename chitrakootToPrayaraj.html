🕉️ Sacred Pilgrimage Sites in Chitrakoot

Gupt Godavari

A pair of mysterious rock caves located in Chitrakoot, Madhya Pradesh. Associated with Lord Ram from the Ramayana, these caves have a natural stream flowing inside them. The caves are believed to be where Lord Ram and Lakshman meditated during their exile.

✨ Mysterious Rock Caves

Sati Anusuya Temple

Sati Anusuya is one of the most celebrated women in Vedic history. The temple is located on the banks of the sacred Mandakini River. According to legend, Anusuya turned the Trimurti (Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva) into infants to save her husband's penance.

🙏 Vedic Heritage Site

Kamtanath Temple / Kamadgiri Mountain

Kamadgiri is the main holy place of Chitrakoot Dham. It is believed to have been the abode of Lord Ram, Sita, and Lakshman during their exile. The Parikrama (circumambulation) around this sacred hill is considered highly meritorious.

🏔️ Main Holy Hill of Chitrakoot

Hanuman Dhara Temple

Lord Ram is believed to have pacified Hanuman here after the Lanka burning. This hilltop temple offers beautiful panoramic views of Chitrakoot. A natural spring flows from the rock where Lord Hanuman is said to have rested.

🌟 Hilltop Temple with Natural Spring

Ram Ghat

Ram Ghat is a highly sacred and peaceful riverfront on the banks of the Mandakini River. Lord Ram, Sita, and Lakshman are believed to have spent part of their exile here. The evening aarti on Ram Ghat is a soul-stirring spiritual experience.

🌊 Sacred Mandakini Riverfront
