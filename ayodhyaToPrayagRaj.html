🛕 Holy Pilgrimage Sites

Ram Janmabhoomi Temple

Magnificent Nagara-style Hindu temple inaugurated in 2024, built on the sacred birthplace of Lord Ram. The divine aura and intricate architecture leave every pilgrim spellbound.

✨ Inaugurated 2024

Hanuman Garhi Temple

Revered temple complex dedicated to Lord Hanuman, believed to be built by saint Baba Bhagwan Das in the 10th century. The 76-step climb leads to immense blessings.

🙏 10th Century shrine

Kanak Bhavan (House of Gold)

Temple dedicated to Lord Ram and Goddess Sita. Legend says it was gifted to Sita by Queen Kaikeyi. The golden idols and intricate carvings are mesmerizing.

🏆 Golden Temple

Dashrath Mahal

Historical site believed to be the palace of King Dashrath, father of Lord Ram. Walk through the ancient remains and feel the epic era of Treta Yuga.

Ram Ki Paidi & Sarayu River

Sacred ghats where Lord Ram took a holy dip before his 14-year exile. Devotees visit for purification and the divine evening aarti — a soul-stirring laser show and spiritual ceremony.

🌟 Evening Aarti + Laser Show
